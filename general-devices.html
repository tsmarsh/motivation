General Tips for Chaos

Over-explainGive long, confusing explanations for simple questions.

Make stuff upReport imaginary spies or dangers. Go full conspiracy mode.

Act clueless Play dumb as much as possible without getting into trouble.

Be annoyingBe irritable, start arguments, but don’t get yourself in too much trouble.

Confuse the rulesAct like you don’t understand the rules around rationing, traffic, or anything else.

Complain about everythingEspecially ersatz (substitute) materials. Nothing’s good enough.

Ice out the enemyTreat Axis nationals and collaborators (Quislings) super cold in public.

Silent treatmentStop talking when Axis nationals or collaborators walk into a room.

Cry on demand Break down into tears over the smallest things, especially in front of government clerks.

Boycott everythingAvoid movies, concerts, newspapers, or anything related to the enemy.

Don’t recycle or salvageIgnore all salvage efforts—don't participate in any of those schemes.
