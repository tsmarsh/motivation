Chaotic from the Office

Mess up ordersGet quantities wrong, confuse names, and send things to the wrong addresses.

Drag out communicationsKeep back-and-forth emails going forever, especially with government offices.

Misfile important stuffPut critical documents in the wrong place so nobody can find them.

Short on copiesWhen making copies, always make one less than needed, so someone has to redo the work./span>

Delay important callsTell important people that the boss is "busy" or on another call, even when they’re not.

Hold onto mailDon't send mail out right away—wait for the next collection.

Spread tea (a little too much)Drop juicy but fake "inside info" rumors to stir up drama.
